About the game
Introducing the first of its kind, an open-source library for web-based motion-contro ed multiplayer games!
Web-Based
Don't bother downloading anything onto your devices. We don't want to hog up any of that precious space. Instead, just simply key in fomosumo.com on your shared screen and mobile browsers and start playing!
MOTION-CONTROLLED
We turned your mobile device into a motion controller. Remember the Nintendo Wii or Switch? Why spend money on those, when the only mobile controller you need is right there in your hands already?
MULTIPLAYER
No more FOMO. Instead of staring at your phone, enjoy a multiplayer experience like no other!
How To Play
It's really simple
Go to fomosumo.com on a shared screen (e.g. computer) and on up to 4 mobile devices.
Using your mobile phone as a motion controller, you and your friends can choose from any of our games, follow the instructions given and start playing!
